1. Zwilling : Twin Layer - Smoothie Finish

Dim : 0'10 X 0'-10 (254 X 254 X 4+11 MM)

About

Zwilling-Twin layer is an economical grade environmental friendly cooling roof tiles. It is Twin layer solid tiles, Top layer 4-5 mm of perlite with fibre reinforcement with Bottom layer 10-11 mm of exfoliated vermiculite mixed with M35 Grade concrete. This is extremely innovative for the building sector and cost effective. This cooling roof tiles on the roof building can reduce inside ceiling temperature by 3-C – 5-C.


Field of Application

Used to provide good thermal insulation for Outdoor applications such as:

  • Residential Buildings
  • Apartments
  • Houses etc.

Characteristics

  • Leak Proof
  • Good heat resistance
  • Provides long energy efficiency
  • Usable Terrace Space
  • Easy installationn

Tiles Installation

  • Tiles should be fixed with cement, sand bed mortar or tile adhesives.

  • Tiles may be laid with a gap of minimum 6mm between each other by using spacers and this gap will ensure proper filling of weather proof / water proof tile grout.
