About Us

VERMIPLAST ® – Cool Roof Tiles (CRT) – We are expertise in Building Architect, Engineers and Design consultants with a twist of 'Green'. We do creative Roof Tile products to our client(s) with beautiful and ingenious green design solutions for building. And also constantly challenge the status quo and keep upping the benchmark by introducing Innovative Sustainable Architectural & Building Solutions.

Specialized Cool Roof tiles (VERMIPLAST ®) are brought to you by a group with over Twenty Three years of experience in the civil construction and building material field. The company is promoted by S.Nandhakumar B.E., IEI, FIV , a graduate in Civil Engineering and specialized in the manufacturing concrete products.


CRT has grown to Five Branches in Tamil Nadu & Karnataka. We are the largest manufacturers of Specialized Cool Roof Tiles in Tamil Nadu.


Being the pioneers in the Industry, We strive for innovation that sets apart from our competition. Our belief in superior Customer Experience resulted in having some of the trusted names in the construction industry as our valuable clients.
